About Agri-Diagnosis

Agri-Diagnosis is an AI-powered platform designed to assist farmers in identifying plant diseases, receiving expert consultations, and making informed decisions to ensure the health of their crops. We are dedicated to providing innovative tools to empower farmers and improve their yield.

Our Key Services

Disease Detection

Upload an image of a diseased plant and receive instant AI-based analysis and recommendations for treatment.

Expert Consultation

Connect with agricultural experts to get personalized advice on managing plant diseases and improving your crop health.

Weather Alerts

Receive timely weather updates and alerts to make informed decisions about spraying, irrigation, and crop care.

Product Recommendations

Find the right pesticides and equipment based on AI analysis and expert advice, all available through partner e-commerce platforms.
