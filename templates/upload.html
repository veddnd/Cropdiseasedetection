Benefits of Early Disease Detection

Increase Yield: Early detection helps prevent crop damage, ensuring higher productivity.

Save Costs: Early action minimizes expensive treatments, saving money.

Eco-Friendly: Reduces pesticide overuse, protecting the environment.

Prevent Spread: Stops diseases from affecting other crops, reducing overall losses.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the detection system work?

Our system uses advanced machine learning models to analyze images of plants and detect diseases based on patterns and visual symptoms.

What should I do if the image is not clear?

Try to capture the infected area in good lighting and ensure it is in focus. A clear image will result in better diagnosis accuracy.

What kind of plants can the system detect?

The system currently supports a wide range of crops, including wheat, rice, maize, and fruits. We are continuously adding support for more crops.
